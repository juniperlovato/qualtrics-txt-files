Original English Version:
Jim spends 2 hours watching TV and then decides to go to bed and reads for half as long. He does this 3 times a week. How many hours does he spend on TV and reading in 4 weeks?

Translated Version:
Giuseppe passa 2 uri a taliari a televisiuni e dopu si curca e leghi pi menzu tempu. Faci chistu pi 3 voti a simana. Quantu uri passa a taliari a televisiuni e a leggiri ntra 4 simani?
