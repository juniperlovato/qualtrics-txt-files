Original English Version:
Mike needed a new pair of jeans. When he got to the mall he saw that his favorite jeans were advertised 25% off. The original price of the jeans was $40. How much money will Mike have left over if he pays with a $50.00 bill?

Translated Version:
Giovanni avia bisognu di na nova para i pantaluna. Quannu arrivau ô centru cummerciali, vidìu ca i soi pantaluna preferuti avìanu nu scontu dû 25%. U prezzu originali era di 40 euro. Quanti sordi ci restanu a Giovanni si paga cu na banconota di 50 euro?
