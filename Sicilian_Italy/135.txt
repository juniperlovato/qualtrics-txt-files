Original English Version:
Gerald works at a daycare that pays him $30 every day. He worked for an entire week and spent a total of $100. How much does he have left?

Translated Version:
Giuseppe travagghia nta na gelateria e pigghia 30 euro ogni jornu. Travagghiò pi tutta na simana e spinnìu in tuttu 100 euro. Quantu soldi ci ristaru?
