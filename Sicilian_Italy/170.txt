Original English Version:
Marilyn wants to buy a pair of boots online. On Amazon.com, the boots cost $16 and shipping costs $4. On eBay, those same pair of shoes are only $13, but shipping costs twice as much as it does on Amazon. How much more expensive do the boots work out to be on eBay?

Translated Version:
Sofia voli accattari un paio di scarpe nuove online. Ncapu a Amazon, li scarpe costanu 16 € e li spisi di spedizioni sunnu 4 €. Ncapu a Zalando, lu stissu paio di scarpe costa sulu 13 €, ma li spisi di spedizioni costanu u doppiu di chiddi di Amazon. Quantu vennu a costari cchiù assai li scarpe ncapu a Zalando?
