Original English Version:
There are 50 books in a small library. Half of them are written in English, and 10% in German. All others are written in Spanish. How many Spanish books are there?

Translated Version:
Nta na picciula biblioteca ci sunnu 50 libbra. A mitati sunnu scritti n'italianu, e u 10% n'ngrisi. Tutti l'autri sunnu scritti n'spagnolu. Quanti libbra spagnoli ci sunnu?
