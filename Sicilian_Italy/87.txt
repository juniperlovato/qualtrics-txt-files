Original English Version:
Marcel runs a bicycle store. Their main products are three types of bikes: MTB, BMX, and Trekking. The price of one MTB is $500, BMX is half the price of an MTB, and a Trekking bike is $450. In one month, they sold a total of 300 bikes among the types listed. Half of them were Trekking bikes, and 15% were BMX bikes. The rest of the sold bikes were MTB type. How much did they earn from selling bicycles during that month?

Translated Version:
Lu signuri Giuseppe gestisci na bottega di bicicletti ntâ so citati. Vinni tri tipi di bicicletti: MTB, BMX e Trekking. Na MTB costa 500 euro, na BMX costa la mità di na MTB, e na Trekking costa 450 euro. Nto misi passatu, Giuseppe vinni n tutali di 300 bicicletti tra sti tipi. La mità di sti bicicletti foru Trekking, e lu 15% foru BMX. Lu restu foru MTB. Quantu guadagnau Giuseppe di tutti sti bicicletti chi vinni n chiddu misi?
