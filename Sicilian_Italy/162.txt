Original English Version:
On Easter Sunday Cindy went to the city park to participate in the Easter Egg Hunt, and ended up with a basket full of eggs. She had 5 green ones, twice as many blue ones as green ones, one fewer pink eggs than blue eggs, and one-third as many yellow eggs as pink eggs. How many eggs did Cindy have altogether?

Translated Version:
Lu jornu di Pasqua, Lucia jiu a lu parcu di la citati pi participari a la caccia di ova di Pasqua e si purtau na panara china di ova. Avia 5 ova verdi, du voti tanti ova blu quante n’avia verdi, na ova rosa di menu di quanti n’avia blu, e na terza parti di ova gialli di quante n’avia rosa. Quanti ova aveva Lucia in tuttu?
