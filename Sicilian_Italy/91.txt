Original English Version:
On Easter Sunday Cindy went to the city park to participate in the Easter Egg Hunt, and ended up with a basket full of eggs. She had 5 green ones, twice as many blue ones as green ones, one fewer pink eggs than blue eggs, and one-third as many yellow eggs as pink eggs. How many eggs did Cindy have altogether?

Translated Version:
A Duminica di Pasqua, a picciridda Sofia stava priparannu un beddu panaru chinu d'ova culurati pâ scampagnata di Pasquetta. Nni misi 5 virdi. Appoi misi l'ova blu, chi èranu lu doppiu di chiddi virdi. L'ova rosa èranu unu 'n menu di chiddi blu. E 'nfinì, l'ova gialli èranu un terzu di chiddi rosa. Quanti ova misi Sofia ntô panaru 'n tuttu?
