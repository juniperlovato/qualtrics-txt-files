Original English Version:
Sean is practicing for their role in a theater production. They have to memorize their lines for two scenes and the lyrics to one solo song. Their solo song has 54 lines in the lyrics. The first scene has twice the number of lines, but only a third of them are their lines. The second scene has six more lines than the song, and four-fifths of them are theirs. How many lines does Sean have to memorize?

Translated Version:
Giuseppe si sta priparannu pi la so parti nta na rapprisintazioni teatrali a scola. Iddu avi a memurizzari li battuti pi dui scenati e puru li versi di na canzuna ca canta sulu. La canzuna avi 54 versi. La prima scenata avi dui voti li versi dâ canzuna, ma sulu na terza sunnu battuti di Giuseppe. La secunna scenata avi sei versi cchiù dâ canzuna, e quattru quinti di chisti sunnu battuti soi. Quanti versi n tutali avi a memurizzari Giuseppe?
