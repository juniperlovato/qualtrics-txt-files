Original English Version:
Ron is fed up with the pothole in front of his house. If it doesn't get fixed, it's going to do $450 worth of damage to his car. Unfortunately, the city council refuses to fix it, and will fine them $120 for unauthorized road maintenance if they fix it themselves. They will also have to buy 3 buckets of asphalt that each cost $25. How much money do they save by fixing the pothole?

Translated Version:
U signuri Salvo non ni po' cchiù dâ granni buca ca c'è davanti a so casa. Si non veni aggiustata, ci fa un dannu di 450 euro â machina. Purtroppo, u Comuni non la voli conzari e, si Salvo si la conza iddu stissu, ci fa na multa di 120 euro pi travagghi non autorizzati. Oltri a chistu, s'havi a accattari 3 sacchi d'asfaltu ca costanu 25 euro l'unu. Quanti soddi risparmia u signuri Salvo si si la conza iddu?
