Original English Version:
Fern is trying to decide between buying the Widgeteer 3000, which makes 60 widgets an hour, and the WidgetMaster 9000, which makes 45 widgets an hour. Each widget sells for $6. How much more money will Fern make from the Widgeteer 3000 vs. the WidgetMaster 9000 if it runs 24 hours a day for a month with 30 days?

Translated Version:
Giovanni voli accattari na machina pi fari cannola. Ci su du tipi: la Cannolatrice 3000, ca fa 60 cannola nta n’ura, e la Cannolamàster 9000, ca fa 45 cannola nta n’ura. Ogni cannolu si veni a 6 euro. Quantu cchiù sordi fa Giovanni cu la Cannolatrice 3000 rispettu a la Cannolamàster 9000 si la machina lavura 24 uri o jornu pi 30 jorna?
