Original English Version:
Jasmine's teacher gives stickers for reward. They was given 15 stickers for participating in class, but they lost 7 stickers during playtime. However, their teacher gave them another 5 stickers for helping her classmates. How many stickers do they have at the end?

Translated Version:
La maestra di Giusy duna figghietti autucollanti comu ricumpensa. Giusy riceviu 15 figghietti autucollanti pi participari a la lezzioni, ma ni persi 7 mentri jucava fora. Poi, la maestra ci nni detti autri 5 pi aiutari li so cumpagni. Quanti figghietti autucollanti havi Giusy a la fini?
