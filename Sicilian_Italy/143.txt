Original English Version:
Morisette and Kael were asked to bring fruits. Morisette brought 5 apples and 8 oranges, while Kael brought twice the amount of apples and half the number of oranges than Morisette. How many fruits do they have in total?

Translated Version:
Carmela e Salvaturi foru addumannati di purtari frutta pâ festa dâ scola. Carmela purtau 5 puma e 8 aranci, mentri Salvaturi purtau u duppiu dî puma e a mità di l'aranci di Carmela. Quanta frutta hannu 'n tuttu?
