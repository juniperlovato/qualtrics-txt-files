Original English Version:
David and Dasha went to the carnival, where there are 9 rides. Each ride costs 2 ride tickets at $2 per ticket. You can also buy a ride bracelet for $30 which gives you 9 rides. If David buys a ride bracelet and Dasha buys tickets, and they ride all 9 rides, how much money does David save?

Translated Version:
Carmelu e Rosalia jeru â festa dû paisi, unni ci sunnu 9 giostre. Ogni giostra costa 2 biglietti ca custanu 2 euru l'unu. Si pò macari accattari nu braccialettu pî giostre a 30 euru ca ti duna u dirittu di fari tutti i 9 giri. Si Carmelu accatta u braccialettu e Rosalia accatta i biglietti, e fannu tutti i 9 giri, quanti sordi arrisparmia Carmelu?
