Original English Version:
Jenny goes to the florist to buy some flowers. Roses cost $2 each and $15 for a dozen. If she bought 15 roses and arrived with five 5 dollar bills and they only have quarters for change, how many quarters does she leave with?

Translated Version:
Giuseppina va ô fioraru pi accattari ciuri. Li rrosa custanu 2 euro ognuna, o 15 euro pi na duzzina. Si accatta 15 rrosa e arrivau cu cincu bancanoti di 5 euro, e ô fioraru hannu sulu muneti di 50 centesimi pi lu restu, quanti muneti di 50 centesimi avi quannu si nni va?
