Original English Version:
Tyler wants to buy a dictionary that costs $18, a dinosaur book that costs $13, and a children's cookbook that costs $8. He has saved $14 from his allowance. If Tyler earns $5 per hour, how many hours does he have to work to afford his books?

Translated Version:
Pippo voli accattari un modellinu di na Fiat 500 ca costa 18€, un libru supra i leggendi siciliani ca costa 13€, e un fumettu ca costa 8€. Iddu havi già misu i latu 14€. Sò nonnu ci duna 5€ ogni vota ca l'aiuta ntô jardinu. Quanti voti havi a aiutari a sò nonnu pi putìrisi accattari tutti sti cosi?
