Original English Version:
Jenny goes to the florist to buy some flowers. Roses cost $2 each and $15 for a dozen. If she bought 15 roses and arrived with five 5 dollar bills and they only have quarters for change, how many quarters does she leave with?

Translated Version:
Sofia va dû fioraio p'accattari i rosi. I rosi cùstanu 2 euru l'una, ma na duzzina, ca su' 12 rosi, custa 18 euru. Sofia n'accatta 15. Si paja cu un bigliettu di 20 euru e unu di 5 euru e u fioraio ci duna u restu sulu cu muniti di 50 centesimi, quanti muniti arricivi?
