Original English Version:
Michael is racing their horse and notices that for every mile the horse runs, they eat 1/2 a bail of hay. A bail of hay costs $3. If their horse runs for 30 minutes at 32MPH, and they have six 5 dollar bills, how much change do they have after they buy the hay?

Translated Version:
Giuseppe sta facennu curriri u so cavallu e nota ca pi ogni chilometru ca u cavallu currì, mancia menzu fasciu di fenu. Un fasciu di fenu costa 3 euro. Si u so cavallu currì pi menzu ura a 50 chilometri all’ura, e iddu avi sei banconote da 5 euro, quantu restu ci resta dopu ca accatta u fenu?
