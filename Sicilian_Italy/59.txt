Original English Version:
Tyson decided to make muffaletta sandwiches for the big game. Each sandwich required 1 pound each of meat and cheese and would serve 4 people. There would be 20 people in total watching the game. The meat cost $7.00 per pound and the cheese cost $3.00 per pound. How much money would they spend on the meat and cheese to make enough sandwiches to serve 20 people?

Translated Version:
U zù Pippu decidi di priparari u pani cunzatu p'u pranzu da duminica cu tutta a famigghia. Pi ogni pani, ci voli menzu chilu di salumi e menzu chilu di furmaggiu, e ogni pani basta pi 4 pirsuni. A manciari sù 20 pirsuni in tutali. I salumi costanu 15 euri o chilu e u furmaggiu costa 8 euri o chilu. Quanti soddi spenni u zù Pippu p'accattari i salumi e u furmaggiu pi priparari abbastanza pani ppi 20 pirsuni?
