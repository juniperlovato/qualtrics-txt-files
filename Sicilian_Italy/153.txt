Original English Version:
Janet’s ducks lay 16 eggs per day. They eat three for breakfast every morning and bakes muffins for their friends every day with four. They sell the remainder at the farmers' market daily for $2 per fresh duck egg. How much in dollars do they make every day at the farmers' market?

Translated Version:
Giuseppina havi quattru addineddi ca ponnu 16 ovu ogni jornu. Ogni matina, si mancia tri ovu pi la colazioni e usa àutri quattru pi fari i biscotti pi li sò amici. Li ovu ca ristanu li porta ogni jornu ô mercatu dû paisi e li veni a 2 euro ognunu. Quanti euro guadagna Giuseppina ogni jornu ô mercatu?
