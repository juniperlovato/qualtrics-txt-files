Original English Version:
The marching band is ordering new uniforms. Each uniform comes with a hat that costs $25, a jacket that costs three times as much as the hat, and pants that cost the average of the costs of the hat and jacket. How much does each uniform cost total?

Translated Version:
A banda musicali dû paisi sta ordinannu divisi novi. Ogni divisa veni cu na coppula chi costa 25 euro, na giacchetta chi costa tri voti cchiù dâ coppula, e pantaluna chi costanu a media dî prezzi dâ coppula e dâ giacchetta. Quantu costa in tuttu ogni divisa?
