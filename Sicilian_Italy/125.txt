Original English Version:
The marching band is ordering new uniforms. Each uniform comes with a hat that costs $25, a jacket that costs three times as much as the hat, and pants that cost the average of the costs of the hat and jacket. How much does each uniform cost total?

Translated Version:
La banda musicale di la scola voli accattari novi divise. Ogni divisa veni cu na coppula ca costa 25 euro, nu giubbotto ca costa tri voti chiossai di la coppula, e na para di pantaluna ca costa la media di li prezzi di la coppula e di lu giubbotto. Quantu costa in tuttu ogni divisa?
