Original English Version:
The marching band is ordering new uniforms. Each uniform comes with a hat that costs $25, a jacket that costs three times as much as the hat, and pants that cost the average of the costs of the hat and jacket. How much does each uniform cost total?

Translated Version:
U gruppu folklorìsticu d'u paisi sta urdinannu i custumi novi p'a festa. Ogni custumi havi na coppola ca custa 25 euro, un gilè ca custa tri voti chiossai d'a coppola, e i pantaluna ca cùstanu a media d'u prezzu d'a coppola e d'u gilè. Quantu custa 'n tutali ogni custumi?
