Original English Version:
Dennis uses 1 pound of butter for every dozen croissants that he makes. He needs to make 6 dozen croissants. The grocery store currently has a promotion for buy one pound of butter get one half off. If the butter costs $4.00 a pound, how much will it cost him to purchase 6 pounds of butter?

Translated Version:
Giovanni usa 1 chilu di burru pi ogni duzina di cornetti ca fa. Iddu havi a fari 6 duzini di cornetti. O supermercatu c'è na prumuzzioni: si accatti un chilu di burru, u secunnu chilu u paga a mità prezzu. Si nu chilu di burru costa 4 euro, quantu spenni in tuttu Giovanni pi accattari 6 chili di burru?
