Original English Version:
Michael is racing their horse and notices that for every mile the horse runs, they eat 1/2 a bail of hay. A bail of hay costs $3. If their horse runs for 30 minutes at 32MPH, and they have six 5 dollar bills, how much change do they have after they buy the hay?

Translated Version:
Peppi sta facennu curriri u so cavaddu pi la festa di Sant'Agata. Nota ca pi ogni chilometru ca curri u cavaddu, mangia 1/2 balla di pagghia. Na balla di pagghia costa 3 euro. Si u cavaddu curri pi 30 minuti a 50 chilometri all'ura, e Peppi avi sei pezzi di 5 euro, quanti soddi ci restanu doppu ca accatta a pagghia?
