Original English Version:
While on vacation in Bali, Thea bought a hat from a craftsman worth $70. If they gave the craftsman four $20 bills, how much change did they get?

Translated Version:
Mentri era in vacanza a Taormina, Thea accattau na capiddina d’un artigianu pi 70 euro. Si detti all’artigianu quattru bancunotti da 20 euro, quantu restu ci detti?
