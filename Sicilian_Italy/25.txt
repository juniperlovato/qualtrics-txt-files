Original English Version:
Marilyn wants to buy a pair of boots online. On Amazon.com, the boots cost $16 and shipping costs $4. On eBay, those same pair of shoes are only $13, but shipping costs twice as much as it does on Amazon. How much more expensive do the boots work out to be on eBay?

Translated Version:
Maria voli accattari na para di scarpi supra Internet. Supra Amazon.it, li scarpi custanu 16 euro e la spedizioni è 4 euro. Supra Subito.it, li stissi scarpi custanu sulu 13 euro, ma la spedizioni custari du voti cchiù assai ca supra Amazon. Quantu cchiù cari vennu li scarpi si Maria li accatta supra Subito.it?
