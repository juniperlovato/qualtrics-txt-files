Original English Version:
John plans to sell all their toys and use the money to buy video games. They have 13 lego sets and he sells them for $15 each. They end up buying 8 video games for $20 each and has $5 left. How many lego sets do they still have?

Translated Version:
Giuvanni voli vìnniri tutti i so pupazzi pi accattari videogiochi. Iddu havi 13 scatuli di Lego e i vìnniri pi 15 euro ognuna. Poi accatta 8 videogiochi a 20 euro ciascunu e ci restanu 5 euro. Quanti scatuli di Lego ci ristaru a Giuvanni?
